Facts About husky

Huskies are an ancient, energetic, and social breed known for their endurance, thick double coats, and unique vocalizations. They can withstand extreme cold and are built for running, with a metabolism that allows them to burn fat for energy without building up lactic acid

A study was conducted on dogs being away from their owner for varying hours and the studies show that dogs who were away from thier owner the longest showed the greatest amount of affection!
